Clear & Transparent Terms

Honest dealings on every project — these terms apply to all our work contracts.

1

All rates quoted are labour rates only, including our machinery & tools (blade & batti). No material is included unless separately specified.

2

Client to provide all material at site free of cost — marble, electric supply, water, working yard, pigment, araldite, feviquick, tinex, washing cloth etc.

100% payment to be released every 15 days against each final bill submitted for work completed at site. No payment delays.

Extra charges will apply for any work not covered in the original quotation. All extras to be agreed in writing before commencement.
